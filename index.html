Karuta Player

This is an auto-reader for the game of Karuta. Upon clicking start, you will hear the opening poem, which is then followed by the 100 poems used in Karuta, Hyakunin Isshu. The order of these poems is completely randomized to mitigate any concerns of player memorization of reading order.

Press the spacebar or the button to pause the reading when resetting card orientation or if you simply want a pause.

To learn more about the game of Karuta, see here, or watch Chihayafuru :).
